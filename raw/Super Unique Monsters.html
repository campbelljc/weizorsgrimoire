Super Unique Monsters

The following table lists levels & treasure classes for Super Unique monsters. Read more about item generation and Treasure Classes.

Since patch 1.10 the level of Super Uniques, Uniques and their minions in Nightmare and Hell difficulties is based on the area level (area level + 3).

TC’s A, B & C are indicated as an estimate of the quality of the items that can be dropped. Treasure Class C is the higher quality drops. Also note that the Treasure Classes A,B and C are specific to each Act. Treasure Class A in Act 2 is better than the Treasure Class A in Act I.

Note the Treasure Class ‘Cx’ for Pindleskin and ThreshSocket, which indicates that they have been nerfed in 1.10.
